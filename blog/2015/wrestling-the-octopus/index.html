Jan 6th, 2015 4:10 pm

Octopress FTW unless…

What a time I’m having trying to wrestle with Octopress. While it’s going to make my life easier, at this point it’s causing sort stress.

Damned sub-modules

Making this beast do what I want is being complicated by the fact tha themes, deploy locations, etc… are all managed via git sub-modules. While that makes it easier for some, I am unfortunately not one of the some. I have to chalk this up to experience if I have any hope of finishing this.

Hopefully this round will work and I won’t continue banging my head against a wall :)

‘Til next time, Bryan
